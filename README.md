This Power BI dashboard analyzes employee productivity and laziness patterns using HR-related data. It helps identify departments and employees that require attention by tracking productivity, social media usage, meeting attendance, missed deadlines, and work mode (WFH/Office).

Objectives
Identify the department with the highest average laziness.
Analyze whether Work From Home (WFH) is associated with higher laziness.
Understand the impact of salary on productivity.
Examine the relationship between social media usage and missed deadlines.
Identify the top-performing employees.
Analyze the impact of meeting attendance on task completion.
Highlight departments that require HR intervention.
Dashboard Features
Interactive slicers for Department, Work Mode, and Salary Level.
KPI cards for Productivity, Laziness Score, and Completion Rate.
Top 10 Most Lazy Employees.
Top 15 Employees with Highest Social Media Usage.
Top 15 Employees with Perfect Completion Rate.
Top 5 Employees with Zero Missed Deadlines.
Department-wise Laziness Analysis.
Drillthrough page for Employee Details.
Conditional formatting to highlight high laziness.
